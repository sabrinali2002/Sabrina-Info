Just Listen

This is the first piece of my series called "the art of music." I used mostly cool colors to create an effect of serenity and it depicts the image of the girl listening to all the music that will be created in the following pieces.

Voice

This is drawn from a photo of my friend who loves to sing, however, as she puts it, doesn’t sing well. Her love for music goes beyond her talents and I admire her for loving something that she believes she isn’t good at.

Passion

I have to say, this drawing took a lot of my time. Each wrinkle would spend me a good 4 minutes, but it was worth it in the end when everything came together. The guitar is an instrument that I greatly want to try and learn for myself in the future, and I always thought that people who play the guitar looked pretty cool. That might have been the reason I drew this in the first place.

Just a bass

Everyone says the bass is like an easier guitar, but I think the bass is one of the most fundamental parts of music. They make up for the base, no pun intended, of every song. I chose to include this seemingly insignificant instrument to showcase that each instrument has their own important part in a song.

Think deep

This is the piece in my concentration that I am most proud of. This is the first pastel drawing I did in the concentration, and I really liked how it turned out. The background being just black and white, I think it makes a stark contrast to all of my other ones filled with color and excitement.

Saratoga

I will have to admit, this is not really a piece I liked. It was way out of my comfort zone, and I just didn’t like the prompt itself because it’s something my dad gave me on a whim. However, I held through till the end, and now, I quite like it.
